Introduction

The EC120 also known as Colibri has its origins in the P120, first launched in 1997. Order's grew fast and production rose to 6 per month by Eurocopter. It features a wide, ergonomic cabin wtih high levels of external visibility which can accomodate pilot with four passengers.

Aircraft

Design

The EC120 Colibri is a single-engine multimission helicopter, designed for safe, simple and cost-effective operations. It incorporates several Eurocopter's features including 8-bladed fenestron® anti-torque tail rotor. The design of the cabin is suitable for variety of civilian and parapublic missions such as utility transport, offshore transport, law enforcement.
According to the manufacturer it integrates a high level of advanced technology to make the rotorcraft easier and safer to fly, as well as reduce costs.

Development

On 20 October 1992, a contract for the join development contract of the new helicopter was signed by three partners of the project, Eurocopter, China National Aero-Technology Import & Export Coropration (CATIC) and Singapore Aerospace Ltd (STAero). Under the join development agreement, Eurocopter received a 61% controlling interest and technical leadre in the programme.

Safety

This is the only single-engine helicopter to memet the requiremenets of the new FAR/JAR 27 safety regulations, which include incorporation of crashworthy fuel systems and energy-attenuating fuel structures and seats.

Technical data

General

EC120 is certified with a pilot being on the right or left side,Fuselage comprising the cabin and the luggage hold with several accesses possibilities (one right rear lateral hinged cargo and one rear access cargo door) and floor tie-down net.
Tubal skid landing gear, with replaceable skid shoes with long footsteps profiler on rear tube, capable of taking handling wheels. Interior signs and marking available in either French or English.

Power plant

One Turbomeca ARRIUS 2F turbine engine complete with starting, fuel supply and governing systems.
One crashworthy fuel system including 2 tanks with a total usable fuel capacity of 406 liters.
One twist throttle with starter button incorporated in collective lever.
Two chip detectors
One engine lubrication and oil cooling system.
One torque-measurement pick-up.

Rotor and flight controls

One main rotor with 3 composite-material around a SPHERIFLEX® titanium rotor head. One anti-torque rotor (FENESTRON) with 8 asymmetrical blades, integrated with vertical fin and three main rotor hydraulic servo units.
