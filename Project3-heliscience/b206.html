Introduction

The most famous helicopter ever made. Bell 206 is a symbol for helicopters everwhere in the world. If you ever seen a helicopter, then you remember these slick lines with a specific nose design that no other helicopter has.

Aircraft

General

The Bell 206 is a familiy of two-bladed, single or twin engined helicopters, manufactured by Bell Helicopter in Mirabel, Queebec. It was designed to be the ideal flying workplace. it can hold up to six passengers with one pilot comfortably and the club-passenger seating allow for face-to-face conversations.

The durable design features the powerful Rolls-Royce 260-C30P engine. The high-inertia two-bladed rotor system provides exceptional stability. All of which make the Bell 206 an outstanding value to your fleet.

Safety

206 is equipped with a variety of safety features including: reliable engines, transmission with chip detection, debris collection, supert autorotatiom , rollover bulkheads, rupture-resistant fuel cells, collective-mounted throttle and optopnal wire strike protection.

Cabin

The interior of the Bell 206 allows for multiple mission configurations. A spacious cabin that provides a comfortable club seating arrangement or specialized equipment insertion for medical and parapublic missions.

Large windows and ample ambient light provides excellent visibility for a working environment inside the cabin with exceptional field of view outside the cabin.

Rotor and flight controls

Main rotor, semi-rigid, two-bladed, see-saw type with precone and underslug feathering axis. All metal blades that are moisture proofed and epoxy encapsulated. Flap restraints.
Tail rotor; semi-rigid, two-bladed, see-saw type.
Hydraulis boost system (pump and reservoir module)
Mechanical flight control linkages throughout
Pilot Cyclic Grip has provisions for Optional Equipment Control.
