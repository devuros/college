Introduction

Roomy, silent and smooth the EC 130 Ecureuil is a single engine helicopter, is one of Eurocopter's best-selling rotorcraft, nothing that 238 orders had been placed in 2011, a 40 per cent increase over the previous year.

Aircraft

Design

EC has two variants: EC130 B4 the initial launch model of the type, and EC 130 T2 upgraded variant of the B4; features redisgned airframe, more powerful engines (Turbomecca Ariel 2D) and increased performance.

This model has bene popular with Air medical services (AMS) operators due to its large cabin being capable of accommodating up to two stretchers and two attendees, as well as a baggage area for storing equipment, wide side doors for access.

Operational history

In 2007, it was reported that the strongest demands for the EC130 and other such single-engine helicopters has come from Latin and North American markets. By May 2008, the EC130 had overtakes the Bell 206 as the best-selling rotorcraft on the market. By 2015, Airbus reported that the EC130 was in service with 279 operators and had accumulated moare than 1,1700,000 total flight hours worldwide!

Technical data

General

The H130 is certified with a pilot being on the left side. Anti-Vibration Control System with 1 controller, actuators and accelerometers. Fuselage comprising the cabin and 3 luagge holds, with floor tie-down nets and access doors. Single color exterior painting. Internal paint: ligh grey (prevailing color).

Power plant

One Turbomecca ARRIEL 2D turbine engines.
One magnetic plug and 1 chip detector.
One fire detection system.
One air-intake protection grid.
One torque-measurement pick-up.

Transmission system

One main gearbox, anti-vibration mounted, with oil sight gauge, chip detector, oil temperature and pressure switches, port for endoscope and self sealing valve for oil sampling and draining.
One main gearbox cooling system.
One engine to main gearbox coupling shaft
One rotor brake
One main rotor high and low r.p.m warning device.
One tail gearbox with oil sight gauge, chip detector and port endoscopic inspection
